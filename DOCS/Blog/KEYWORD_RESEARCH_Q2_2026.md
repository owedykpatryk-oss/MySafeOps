## Notes on search intent shifts (April 2026)

Based on current SERP analysis:

- **"Software" keywords** are increasingly dominated by AI-driven content — differentiation requires real expertise signals (HSE codes, case studies, actual numbers)
- **"UK" suffix** still matters for filtering out US/global SERP results — add to every target keyword
- **Video embeds** increasingly appear in top 3 — consider adding short explainer videos where feasible
- **Reddit/community** results creeping up for "how to" queries — informational content must be more authoritative than forum threads
- **FAQ rich snippets** have expanded significantly in UK SERP since January 2026 — our schema markup strategy is well-timed
