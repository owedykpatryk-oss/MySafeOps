# MySafeOps — Brand Guide

## Identity
- **Name:** MySafeOps
- **Domain:** mysafeops.com
- **Tagline:** Site safety, simplified.
- **Full:** RAMS & Permit System
- **Audience:** UK construction companies, site managers, H&S professionals

## Logo
The logo is a teal shield with an orange checkmark — representing protection (shield) and compliance (check).

### Files
| File | Usage |
|------|-------|
| `logo-horizontal.svg` | Main logo — website header, emails, documents |
| `logo-horizontal-dark.svg` | For dark backgrounds — app header, dark sections |
| `logo-stacked.svg` | Square format — PDF cover pages, social media |
| `logo-icon.svg` | App icon, favicon, small spaces |

### Clear space
Minimum clear space around logo = height of the "M" in MySafeOps on all sides.

### Minimum sizes
- Horizontal: min width 120px
- Stacked: min width 60px
- Icon: min 24px

### Don'ts
- Don't change the colours
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't add effects (shadows, outlines)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional** but not corporate
- **Direct** — say what you mean, no jargon
- **Construction-savvy** — we know sites, we speak the language
- **UK-focused** — British English, UK regulations, UK examples
- **Helpful** — not salesy, not pushy

### Do say:
- "Built for UK construction sites"
- "Workers use it for free"
- "UK H&S law compliant"
- "From your phone, on site"

### Don't say:
- "Revolutionary AI-powered solution"
- "Synergistic compliance ecosystem"
- "Best-in-class digital transformation"
