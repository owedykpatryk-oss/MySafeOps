Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

MySafeOps ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

MySafeOps is a construction safety management platform operated from the United Kingdom. For the purposes of data protection law, we are the data controller.

Contact: privacy@mysafeops.com

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you register, we collect: name, email address, phone number, company name, and role (admin/supervisor/worker).

Worker data

Organisations may store worker information including: name, role, company, phone, CSCS card details, certificate numbers and expiry dates, emergency contacts, and medical information (if provided voluntarily).

Project data

Documents created in the platform including: RAMS, permits to work, site reports, incident reports, risk assessments, method statements, checklists, and inspection records.

Photos and files

Site photographs (which may include GPS location data), uploaded documents, certificate scans, site plan drawings, and company logos.

Vehicle and equipment data

Vehicle registrations, MOT/insurance/tax dates, equipment serial numbers, inspection records, and calibration certificates.

Technical data

IP address, browser type, device information, and usage analytics. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

  • Contract performance — to provide the MySafeOps service you've signed up for
  • Legal obligation — to help organisations comply with UK health and safety legislation (HASAWA 1974, CDM 2015, RIDDOR 2013, etc.)
  • Legitimate interest — to improve our service, prevent fraud, and provide support
  • Consent — for marketing communications (you can unsubscribe at any time)

4. How we store and protect it

All data is stored on Cloudflare's global network infrastructure. Cloudflare is SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 certified, and GDPR compliant.

  • Database: Cloudflare D1 (encrypted at rest)
  • Files: Cloudflare R2 (encrypted at rest, no public access by default)
  • Passwords: hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • API: all traffic encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Access: role-based permissions (admin, supervisor, worker)

5. Who has access

  • Your organisation's admin users can see all data within your organisation
  • Supervisors can see project data they are assigned to
  • Workers can only see documents relevant to them and their own certificates
  • MySafeOps staff may access data only for support purposes, with your permission
  • We never sell, share, or give your data to third parties for marketing

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. UK health and safety law requires certain records to be kept for specific periods:

  • RAMS and risk assessments: minimum 3 years (recommended 6 years)
  • Accident records: 3 years (6 years for RIDDOR)
  • Health surveillance records: 40 years
  • COSHH exposure records: 40 years

When you delete your account, we will delete all personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — for any consent-based processing

To exercise any of these rights, email: privacy@mysafeops.com

8. GPS and location data

When workers take photos using MySafeOps, the app may record GPS coordinates. This is used solely for documenting site conditions and is stored with the photo. Location tracking is only active during photo capture — we do not continuously track worker locations.

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the app to function (authentication tokens). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No third-party analytics that track individual users.

10. Children

MySafeOps is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

12. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us first at privacy@mysafeops.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
