Cookie policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes how MySafeOps uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit mysafeops.com or use the web application.

1. What we use

We use cookies and browser storage that are strictly necessary for the service to work, including:

  • Session and authentication tokens when you choose cloud sign-in (Supabase)
  • Local preferences (for example theme, last-opened workspace) stored in your browser
  • Security-related values (for example sign-in attempt lockout counters) where applicable

2. Analytics and marketing

We do not use third-party advertising cookies on the product. If we add optional analytics in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent before non-essential cookies are set.

3. Managing cookies

You can clear site data and cookies from your browser settings at any time. Doing so may sign you out of the cloud account and reset local preferences.

4. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@mysafeops.com
